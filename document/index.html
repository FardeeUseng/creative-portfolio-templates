Sections Overview

Everything you need to build a compelling portfolio.

home

Hero Section

A visually striking introduction with a clear headline, subheading, and prominent call-to-action buttons to guide visitors.

person

About Me

Detail your professional background, skills, and passion for development. Includes a space for a professional headshot.

work

My Projects

A filterable gallery to display your projects. Each project can have a title, description, technology stack, and links to live demos or code repositories.

reviews

Testimonials

Build credibility by showcasing positive feedback from clients or colleagues in a clean, easy-to-read slider format.

email

Contact

A simple and effective contact form for potential clients or employers to get in touch, complete with validation.

horizontal_rule

Footer

Contains your social media links, a copyright notice, and quick navigation links to important sections of your portfolio.
